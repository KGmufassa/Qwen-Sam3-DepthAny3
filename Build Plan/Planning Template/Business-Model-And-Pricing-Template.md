# Business Model And Pricing Template

## Purpose

Use this document when the app has commercial intent.

It defines how the product creates value, how that value is monetized, what usage boundaries matter, and what pricing assumptions must be true for the MVP to make business sense.

## 8. Freeze Rule

The business model is considered frozen when:

- the revenue model is approved
- usage boundaries are defined
- pricing implications are understood
- the MVP scope and stack align with the chosen model

If the business model changes later, reopen:

- MVP scope planning
- analytics planning
- security and billing-related planning
- any affected slice implementation plans
